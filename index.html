~ WORDIE ~

Instructions

The aim of Wordie is to guess the correct word, so to begin, simply enter any five-letter word and look at the results.

The game play is as follows:

  1. Shoot a word as a guess for the hidden daily word.
  2. Any letters which are within the hidden target word are colored in yellow.
  3. Any letters which match exactly the letter in the hidden target word are colored green.
  4. Tinker a new candidate word as a guess for the hidden target word, and go back to Step 1
